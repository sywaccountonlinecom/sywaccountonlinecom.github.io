2. How do I log in to my SYW Credit Card account?

6. Is my SYW account login secure?

Yes. The portal uses encryption and supports two-factor authentication (2FA) to ensure your account and payment data remain safe.

7. How can I contact SYW customer support?

You can reach customer service through the support section inside your SYW account or on the Shop Your Way Credit Card help page. They assist with login issues, payments, and account locks.

8. Can I activate alerts for payments and rewards?

Yes. Go to “Alerts & Notifications” in your dashboard and enable reminders for payments, statement availability, and rewards milestones.
